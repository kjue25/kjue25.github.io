What does our heating pillows do?

At Malie, the idea of this warming pillow is to soothe and relax. Filled with rice and lavender buds, or an option of rice and essential oils. Our product only takes 10-20 seconds to warm up in the microwave. You can also freeze it to become an ice pack.

Potential Uses

  • Meditation Pillow: Relax and place our heating pillow anywhere on your body after a long day of work. This is a perfect gift for people who love to do yoga or any form of activity.

  • Eye Soother: Use our product to reduce the tension and unclog a congested area. Use our product like a weighted blanket.

  • Pain Relief: If there is a place hurting, heat up this pillow. Our product is a great choice to help sore areas of your body, as well as cramps.

  • Our product can be put in the freezer as well! The pillow is not only for people who like warmth, but those who enjoy the cool. If you like to relax and sleep cooler, this is the product for you!
