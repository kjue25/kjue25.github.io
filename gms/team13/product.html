Calendar Magnets

Calendar magnets are a fun and easy way to keep track of the date anywhere you go. Our calendars can stick to anything with magnetic material, your computer, your whiteboard, your window frame and even your fridge! Because our calendars are small you can take them on the go! You can pick them to have solid colors, black and white and we even have a set where each month is decorated to look like a holiday that gets celebrated in that month!
